# Sunstone Advisory Group — Brand System

*Source: official Sunstone branding kit, April 2026*

## Identity

- **Company name:** Sunstone Advisory Group
- **Tagline:** Federal Market Intelligence & Lobbying
- **Service lines:**
  - Federal Contracting Support (registration, capture, proposals)
  - M&A Advisory for government contractors (buy-side / sell-side)
  - Federal Grant Support (discovery, applications, compliance)
  - Combining AI-powered analytics with established DC relationships

## Color Palette

| Hex | Role | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| `#2a2622` | Primary (dark warm brown-black) | Logo background, BLUF block, document headers |
| `#000000` | Secondary (true black) | Body text, primary text |
| `#f5f4ef` | Secondary (warm cream / off-white) | Page background, section dividers, subtle areas |
| `#f0a742` | Secondary (Sunstone orange) | Accent, CTAs, key emphasis, persona icon accents |
| `#ffffff` | Secondary (pure white) | Type on dark backgrounds, contrast areas |

## Typography

**Font stack:** `Inter, "Helvetica Neue", sans-serif`

- Body: 16px, weight 400
- UI: 14px, weight 500-600

## Document Standards

- BLUF (Bottom Line Up Front) section at top of every analytical document
  - Background: `#2a2622` (primary dark)
  - Text: `#ffffff` (white)
  - Larger than body text
- Page background: `#f5f4ef` (cream) or `#ffffff` (white) depending on context
- Body text: `#000000`
- Accent / emphasis: `#f0a742` (orange)
- Headers and subheads: `#2a2622` (dark)

## Application

- All client-facing deliverables use this palette
- Lamb Packages (Steptoe Briefs) use the Steptoe Brief icon + full Sunstone branding
- Report covers feature the Sunstone medallion + audience-specific persona icon
- White papers leave-behind to agencies: full Sunstone branding for credibility
